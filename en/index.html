about me

a multidisciplinary journey,
a unique perspective

I built my professional path across very different sectors: from mechanical design in the automotive industry to driving vehicles in urban and airport transport, through logistics administration and visual communication.

This breadth was not accidental. It reflects a natural inclination toward continuous learning and the ability to adapt to different operational contexts, always maintaining a methodical, results-oriented approach.

Over time I have complemented traditional technical skills with a genuine interest in emerging digital tools: from artificial intelligence to blockchain, from vector graphics to web development — not to follow trends, but to broaden my ability to contribute effectively.
